About Me

Hello! I'm K S Sooraj, a passionate UI/UX designer who approaches every project like crafting a book. Just as a great book captivates with its cover and holds attention through its thoughtful content, I believe exceptional digital experiences start with stunning, intuitive visuals (the "cover") and are sustained by seamless, meaningful interactions (the "pages").

I pay equal attention to aesthetics and functionality — ensuring designs are not only beautiful but also user-centered, accessible, and delightful to explore. Inspired by typography, layout, and storytelling in literature, I bring that same depth and care to interfaces.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me lost in a good book or sketching new ideas.
